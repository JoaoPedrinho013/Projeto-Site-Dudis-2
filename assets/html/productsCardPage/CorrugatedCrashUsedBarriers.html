    Corrugated Crash Used Barriers

    These corrugated crash barriers are brand used. They are 12″
    deep, and the overall length is 3.5m but once they are
    overlapped to be joined or indeed a post is placed directly
    behind where they join. Their effective length is 3.2m. They have a
    multitude of uses, including in the industrial/domestic market
    where they have many uses for traffic segregation, car parking
    and retaining walls. They can also be used in the agricultural
    market for ideal for making cattle pens.
